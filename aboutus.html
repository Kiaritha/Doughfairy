About us

Founders

The bakery was started in 2019 in Nakuru town by Ms.Veronicah Maina who is passionate about baking.
She became passionate about it ever since she was young after seeing her mother and father running their bakery
for years in her childhood. She learned how to bake from her parents and eventually from attending various baking masterclasses
from professional bakers. Due to this she has already gannered enough skills to bake nd run a bakery effectively.

Quality assurance

Due to the expertise of the founder and the team behind her, Dough fairy guarantees the freshest and most
flavourful cakes in the city. They are baked with love and dedication with the customer in mind.
